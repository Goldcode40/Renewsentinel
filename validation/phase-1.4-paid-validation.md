# RenewSentinel — Phase 1.4 (Paid Validation: Google Search)

## Goal (what success looks like)
- Target CPL (cost per lead): – (early B2B, small volume is OK)
- Target lead-to-call rate: 20–40%
- Target call-to-paid (founding offer) rate: 10–25%
- Budget + duration (test window): /day for 7 days (=) then adjust

## Conversion flow (keep it simple)
1) Google Search ad
2) Landing page (Phase 1.2)
3) Lead magnet opt-in (Phase 1.3)
4) Confirmation email (instant delivery)
5) Manual follow-up to close Founding Offer

## Tracking (minimum viable)
- Use UTM params:
  - utm_source=google
  - utm_medium=search
  - utm_campaign=nh-hvac-validation
  - utm_content={adgroup}
  - utm_term={keyword}
- Record leads in:
  - A simple Google Sheet: Date, Name, Email, Shop, State, Gas/Oil/Refrigerant, Source/UTM, Notes, Status
- What counts as a “qualified lead”:
  - Works for (or owns) an HVAC shop
  - In NH (or nearby state) OR clearly has compliance/COI/permit needs
  - Mentions tracking expirations, COIs, licenses/certs, inspections, permits, or “proof requests”

## Follow-up script (email/SMS)
- First reply (5 lines max):
  - Thanks — quick question so I can send the right setup:
  - Are you in NH, and do you do (1) gas work, (2) oil heat, (3) refrigerants?
  - Roughly how many techs need license/cert tracking?
  - If you want, we can set up your first 10 items and generate your first Proof Pack.
- If they respond "yes":
  - Perfect. The Founding Offer is  concierge setup + 30 days free (first 10 NH shops).
  - What’s a good email + phone, and when can we do a 15-min setup call?
  - If you prefer, reply with your current COI + list of tech names and we’ll prep it before the call.
- If they say "later":
  - No problem — I’ll send the checklist + tracker and a reminder in 7 days.
  - If a GC/customer asks for proof before then, reply “PROOF” and we’ll help you generate a pack.

## Stop/Keep rules (hard gates)
- Stop if:
  - After  spend: < 2 qualified leads total OR CPC is high and queries are mostly “free/template” despite negatives
- Keep going if:
  - >= 3 qualified leads in 7 days AND at least 1 agrees to a call
- Iterate if:
  - Leads are coming but unqualified: tighten keywords to exact match + add more negatives
  - Clicks but no opt-ins: strengthen above-the-fold + move CTA higher + add “Checklist + Tracker” as the immediate value
  - Too expensive: narrow location + shift budget to the better ad group + cap CPC harder
