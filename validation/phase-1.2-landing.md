# RenewSentinel — Phase 1.2 (Landing + Offer Spec)

## Goal (single conversion event)
- Primary CTA: Sign up for early access
- Secondary CTA (optional): “See how we solve compliance tracking for HVAC businesses”

## Target (from Phase 1.1)
- HVAC shop owner / office manager

## Above-the-fold (must pass the 5-second test)
### Headline (one line)
- "Never lose a job to an expired license or missing proof."

### Subheadline (1–2 lines)
- "Track your HVAC licenses, insurance, and certifications automatically. Produce your compliance proof in minutes."

### Bullets (3 bullets max, outcome-driven)
- Track compliance dates (license/insurance/cert expiry)
- Instant access to Proof Pack for GCs/customers
- Stay compliant and avoid downtime or fines

### CTA button text
- "Get early access now"

### Trust line (one short line, no fake claims)
- "Used by HVAC businesses in regulated states like NH."

## Who it's for / Who it's not (tight)
**For:**
- HVAC shops with 1–10 employees needing compliance tracking
- Contractors needing proof of licenses, COIs, and insurance

**Not for:**
- Large HVAC businesses with existing compliance departments
- Shops not interested in managing compliance digitally

## The “Proof Pack” section (what they get)
- What a Proof Pack includes:
  - COI, license, and cert documents
  - Expiry dates + reminders
  - Optional permit/inspection receipts
- Why it matters (in their words):
  - "We can send the full package with one click — no more scrambling."

## How it works (3 steps)
1. Sign up and enter your HVAC shop details
2. We pull your compliance items and set reminders for expiration
3. Generate your Proof Pack in one click when needed

## Objections (answer the top 5)
1. “We already track compliance in a spreadsheet.”
   - RenewSentinel automates reminders and Proof Pack generation. Never miss an expiration.
2. “Seems like extra work.”
   - Setup is quick (5 minutes). After that, it’s reminders, not management.
3. “How do I know this will actually help?”
   - One avoided shutdown or missed job pays for months of use.
4. “Is this just for the business or the techs too?”
   - Both. Track compliance for your business and each technician.
5. “Do I have to upgrade to get the full functionality?”
   - Start with the basic compliance tracker, then upgrade to get Proof Pack export and more.
