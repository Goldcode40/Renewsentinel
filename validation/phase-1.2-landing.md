# RenewSentinel — Phase 1.2 (Landing + Offer Spec)

## Goal (single conversion event)
- Primary CTA: Collect qualified leads for a Founding Offer (paid onboarding / early access)
- Secondary CTA (optional): “See what’s in a Proof Pack” (scroll link)

## Target (from Phase 1.1)
- NH HVAC shop owner / office manager

## Above-the-fold (must pass the 5-second test)
### Headline (one line)
- Never lose a job to an expired license or missing proof.

### Subheadline (1–2 lines)
- RenewSentinel tracks the compliance that stops HVAC work (licenses, certs, insurance, permits) and generates a 1-click Proof Pack you can hand to a GC, customer, or inspector.

### Bullets (3 bullets max, outcome-driven)
- Stop surprise expirations (you get notified early, with a clear “what’s expiring next” list)
- Prove compliance fast (export a Proof Pack in minutes, not hours)
- Reduce office chaos (one place for dates, docs, and who’s covered)

### CTA button text
- Get the Founding Offer

### Trust line (one short line, no fake claims)
- Early access for NH HVAC shops — help shape the product, lock in founder pricing.

## Who it's for / Who it's not (tight)
**For:**
- HVAC shops that do service + installs and need to keep licenses/certs/COIs current
- Owners/office managers tired of spreadsheets, sticky notes, and “oh no” renewals

**Not for:**
- Large enterprises with an existing compliance department + complex ERP
- Shops that don’t need to show proof to anyone (no GCs, no permits, no inspections)

## The “Proof Pack” section (what they get)
- What a proof pack includes:
  - Your current COI(s) (GL / WC / Auto as applicable)
  - License/cert list for the work scope (e.g., fuel gas / oil heat / EPA 608)
  - Permit/inspection receipts or job compliance notes (optional add-on per job)
- Why it matters (in their words):
  - “The GC asked for proof and we needed it today.”
  - “We can’t risk a shutdown because someone’s credential lapsed.”

## How it works (3 steps)
1. Add your shop + licensed techs (or we do it with you in 20 minutes)
2. Select what you need to track (NH HVAC starter list included)
3. Upload docs once → get reminders + export Proof Packs anytime

## Objections (answer the top 5)
1. “We already use a spreadsheet.”
   - Great — RenewSentinel is the spreadsheet that actually nags you, stores proof, and exports packs without scrambling.
2. “This feels like extra admin work.”
   - Setup is fast (starter list + guided wizard). After that it’s maintenance, not management.
3. “What if the rules change?”
   - We start narrow (NH HVAC) and expand. Your items are yours either way — you can track anything with an expiry.
4. “Is this for the company or each tech?”
   - Both. Org-level insurance/docs + person-level licenses/certs, tied together in Proof Packs.
5. “How do I know this will pay off?”
   - One avoided shutdown / missed job / last-minute scramble pays for months.
