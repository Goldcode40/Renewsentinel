# RenewSentinel — Phase 1.1 (Wedge Spec)

## Target customer (who exactly)
- HVAC shop owner/office manager (1–10 employees) in regulated states (starting with NH)
- They handle (or should handle) license renewals, insurance COIs, and proof requests from GCs, customers, and inspectors.

## Painful outcome (what they fear/lose)
- “We showed up to a job and got stopped” (inspection fails / permit issues / missing licensed tech for gas/oil scope).
- Fines, rework, and schedule chaos when a credential is expired or the wrong person is assigned.
- Lost revenue and reputation when a GC/customer asks for proof (COI/license/cert) and they can’t produce it fast.

## One-sentence wedge promise
- RenewSentinel keeps your HVAC shop compliant (licenses + certs + insurance + permits) so you never lose a job to an expiration — and you can produce proof in minutes.

## Top 10 compliance items (starting narrow; NH HVAC first)
> Example NH HVAC compliance list (keep it **simple** for now to narrow scope):
1. NH Fuel Gasfitter license
2. NH Oil Heating Technician license
3. EPA Section 608 Technician Certification
4. General Liability insurance policy + COI
5. Workers’ Compensation policy (if employees)
6. Commercial auto policy (if using service vehicles)
7. Business registration / entity status proof
8. Permits & inspections per job
9. Job-required proof packs (GC/customer requests)
10. Licensing & certification list per technician

## What we are NOT doing yet (anti-scope list)
- No subcontractor portal yet
- No OCR email parsing yet
- No calendar sync yet
- No Stripe integration yet
